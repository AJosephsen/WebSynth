How to Play

Flip the illuminated power switch to boot the synth. Use keyboard keys A, S, D, F, G, H, J, K to play notes. Change octaves with the module buttons or tap Z (down) / X (up). Dial in oscillator, filter, and reverb to sculpt vintage tones.
